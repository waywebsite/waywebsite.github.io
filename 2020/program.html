Who Are You?! Adventures in Authentication Workshop (WAY)

Location

WAY 2020 will take place as a virtual event on Friday, August 7th, 2020 from 10am-2pm Eastern Time (ET) on Zoom. If you want to participate, you can register here.

Slack

Get involved, meet your peers, discuss important issues: Join our Slack.

Program

Instead of streaming pre-recorded videos of the talks, we decided that we wanted to maintain WAY's interactive nature even though this means we needed to accept the risk of technical difficulties and connectivity issues. We may reorder talks within sessions to adapt in the case of technical issues (e.g., if the first speaker in a session has a technical problem, we may ask the second speaker to present while that problem is being resolved). We plan to allow 15 minutes per paper, with a maximum of 10 minutes used for the presentation, leaving at least 5 minutes for discussion and some extra time for setting up the screen sharing in Zoom. Instead of splitting the program by topic, we arranged WAY's program this year by the speaker's location.

Note: There is no registration deadline, you can join anytime, even on Friday during the workshop. Just register and you will receive an email with all required details.
