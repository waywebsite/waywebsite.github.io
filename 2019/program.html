Program

Who Are You?! Adventures in Authentication Workshop (WAY)

Location

WAY will be held in Room 3 of the Hyatt Regency Santa Clara.

Note: WAY 2019 is co-located with the Symposium on Usable Privacy and Security (SOUPS).
